On the writer

About

ia

I write to understand the parts of myself
that don't speak plainly.

My poems are fragments of longing, clarity, confusion, and small moments of truth. This space is where I gather them — not to impress, but to witness.

I believe language is one of the few tools we have for the impossible work of understanding ourselves. Every poem I write is an attempt to translate something that lived silently in me into something that can be seen, held, shared.

This site is a sanctuary — for me, and for anyone who needs a quiet place to breathe. You are welcome here.

"Here, language is a doorway. Here, you are allowed to feel."
